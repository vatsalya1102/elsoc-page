ELSOC- Society of Electrical Engineering Department


ELSOC - The Society of Electrical Engineering Department, National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, is a society which aims to cultivate curiosity and interest towards Electrical Engineering in the college. The society conducts various engaging events like workshops and seminars on the latest developments in electrical engineering and other various topics that affect the career path of students. Our recent events include the very successful “Intern Talk” and “Placementoria” where students were provided guidance regarding the research internship opportunities in public as well as private sector. With a team of highly dedicated and qualified individuals, ELSOC is driven with ambition to change the mindset of students towards Electrical Engineering and make it something that they genuinely enjoy.
